Interactive Plaza

There is a program with interactive interface based on StyleGan2 and p5.js. The change speed of the power image generated by StyleGan2 is controlled through gesture detection. When the speed of human gesture manipulation is faster, the algorithm "archive" on the screen will also run faster. However, when the human hands are closed, the "archive" will stop running and save the picture in the background for the audience to print, leaving the power in the eyes of the algorithm.
